## 5. Naming Rules for Forks

If you distribute a modified version of RECURSUM:

- Choose a **distinct name** that does not include "RECURSUM" (unless you have permission)
- Include a clear disclaimer:

> "This is an independent fork and is not affiliated with or endorsed by NeuroTechNet S.A.S."

**Example acceptable names:**

- ✅ "MyProject (based on RECURSUM)"
- ✅ "FastRecurrence" (with attribution in README)

**Example unacceptable names:**

- ❌ "RECURSUM Pro"
- ❌ "RECURSUM Enterprise"
- ❌ "NeuroTechNet RECURSUM"
